Garbriel García Márquez

Gabriel José de la Concordia García Márquez (6 March 1927 – 17 April 2014) was a Colombian novelist, short-story writer, screenwriter and journalist. He is considered one of the most significant authors of the 20th century and one of the best in the Spanish language.

Notable Works

  • One Hundred Years of Solitude
  • The Autumn of the Patriarch
  • Love in the Time of Cholera
  • Chronicle of a Death Foretold

Notable Awards

  • Neustadt International Prize for Literature (1972)
  • Nobel Prize in Literature (1982)
